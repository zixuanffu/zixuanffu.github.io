About Me

Bonjour! I am an MRes student at the Toulouse School of Economics, where I struggle with coming up with research ideas. Acknowledgement to one of the greatest supervisors, Prof. Thierry Magnac, who has put up with my mistakes. Here is my CV, which is currently unavailable because I am ashamed of sharing it with the world at the moment.

My research has not started yet, but my interests are ever-evolving among behavioral economics, mechanism design, the economics of education, and empirical Bayes. I should probably include topics in macroeconomics when I am looking for a job. Anyway, my interests are more fascinating than what my mind can currently offer.

I have spent a little less than 4 years at HKUST, which has crystal-clear skies and a sapphire-blue sea. I also lived in the ever-expensive yet magnificent Lausanne, Switzerland, during a semester exchange at EPFL. Thanks to these experiences, I can speak fluent Cantonese and French at restaurants and supermarkets :) If you are interested in chatting with me, feel free to drop me an email. (My official TSE email address is not available yet because no one is working from the end of July to the end of August :)
