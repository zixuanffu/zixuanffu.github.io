About Me

Bonjour! I am an MRes + Doctoral student at the Toulouse School of Economics, where I struggle with coming up with research ideas. Acknowledgement to one of the greatest supervisors, Prof. Thierry Magnac, who has put up with my silliness. Here is my CV, which I am a bit ashamed of sharing it with the world.

My research has not started yet, but my interests are ever-evolving among behavioral economics, mechanism design, the economics of education, and empirical Bayes. Anyway, my interests are more fascinating than what my mind can currently offer.

I have spent a little less than 4 years at HKUST, which has crystal-clear skies and a sapphire-blue sea. I also lived in the ever-expensive yet magnificent Lausanne, Switzerland, during a semester exchange at EPFL. Thanks to these experiences, I can speak fluent Cantonese and French at restaurants and supermarkets :) If you are interested in chatting with me, feel free to drop me an email.
